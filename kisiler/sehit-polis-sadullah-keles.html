Şehit Polis Sadullah Keleş

Neden bu isim verildi?

Kars’ın Cumhuriyet Mahallesi’nde bulunan Şehit Polis Sadullah Keleş Sokak, ismini görev sırasında vatan savunmasında hayatını kaybeden bir polis memurundan alır. Bu adlandırma; gösterdiği fedakârlığı ve görev bilincini ölümsüzleştirmek, toplumsal hafızada sürekli yaşatmak amacı taşır.

Martyr Police Officer Sadullah Keleş

Detailed information about exactly where and when Martyr Police Officer Sadullah Keleş was martyred is not clearly available in publicly accessible sources.

Reason for the name

Şehit Polis Sadullah Keleş Street, located in the Cumhuriyet Neighborhood of Kars, takes its name from a police officer who lost his life while defending the homeland during duty. The purpose of naming this street is to immortalize his sacrifice and sense of duty and to keep his memory alive in the collective memory.
