Neden Tuncer GÜVENSOY Caddesi?

Kars’taki bu caddeye Tuncer Güvensoy adı verilmesinin sebebi,onun kent içinde tarihi yapıların ve kültürel mirasın korunmasına yaptığı katkılar ve Kars’ın kültürel yaşamına sağladığı hizmetlerdir.

English

Tuncer Güvensoy is a businessman from Kars. He was born in Kars in 1952 and completed his primary, middle, and high school education in his hometown. In 1974, he graduated from Karadeniz Technical University. He worked at the General Directorate of State Hydraulic Works (DSİ).

At the same time, he contributed to the preservation of historical buildings and heritage in Kars. His property, known as the “Tuncer Güvensoy House,” plays an important role in the city’s cultural heritage. This historic building is kept open for visitors by his wife, Hatice Güvensoy, in order to honor and preserve his memory.

Why Tuncer GÜVENSOY Street?

The reason this street in Kars was named after Tuncer Güvensoy is his contributions to the preservation of historical buildings and cultural heritage in the city, as well as his services to Kars’s cultural life.
